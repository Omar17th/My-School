About us

Bangladesh International School and College, Nirjhor is an educational institution located in Nirjhor, Dhaka Cantonment, Dhaka, Bangladesh. It is operated by the Bangladesh Army. The institution was officially established in 2015.

As of 2025, the chairman is Brigadier General A F M Atiqur Rahman. And, the principal is Lt Col K M Zahidul Hasan. The institution's Educational Institute Identification Number (EIIN) is 138186. Both boys and girls can study at the institution.
