About us

Bangladesh International School and College, Nirjhor[a][1][2][3] is an educational institution located in Nirjhor, Dhaka Cantonment, Dhaka, Bangladesh. It is operated by the Bangladesh Army.[4] The institution was officially established in 2015.[5][6][7][8]

As of 2025, the chairman[b] is Brigadier General A F M Atiqur Rahman.[5] And, the principal[c] is Lt Col K M Zahidul Hasan. The institution's Educational Institute Identification Number (EIIN) is 138186.[5][9][better source needed] Both boys and girls can study at the institution.
